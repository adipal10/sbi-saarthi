# SBI Saarthi — Video Voiceover Script
### "Ramesh's Story" · ~2 min 25 sec · for Canva / Filmora

**How to use:** Record the reel in Present mode (press `P` then `F`), then read each scene's VO over it. The on-screen captions are already burned into the video — keep your VO close to them but don't read them word-for-word. Timings are the scene durations set in the reel (`data-dur`); adjust if your delivery runs long.

**Tone:** warm, calm, documentary — like you're telling one man's story, not pitching a product. Slow down on Ramesh's pain; lift and quicken when Saarthi arrives.

---

### 1 · HOOK — 7s  *(cold, mysterious)*
> "Half a billion Indians have a bank account. Most never use it for more than a balance check. **This is the story of one of them.**"

*Captions:* 500 million bank accounts → Most, barely used → This is one story

---

### 2 · THE PROBLEM — 11s  *(cold)*
> "India has **six-point-three crore** small businesses. Only **fourteen percent** can get formal credit. They earn on UPI, they file GST — yet to the bank, they're **invisible**. A **twenty-five lakh crore** rupee gap."

*Captions:* 6.3 crore small businesses → Only 14% can get credit → Creditworthy — yet invisible → A ₹25 lakh crore gap

---

### 3 · THE GAP — 9s  *(cold)*
> "And the ones already inside? **Seventy-four million** YONO users — most only check a balance. Acquire, adopt, engage: **three broken systems, one missing link.**"

*Captions:* 74M YONO users, mostly idle → Acquire · Adopt · Engage → Three systems, one missing link

---

### 4 · MEET RAMESH — 12s  *(pain — slow down, let it breathe)*
> "Meet **Ramesh**. He runs a kirana store. His shop lives on UPI — but he has no credit history, the forms are in English, and every bank has said **no**. The system simply **cannot see him**."

*Captions:* Meet Ramesh → His shop runs on UPI → No credit history · English forms → Every bank said no → Invisible to the system

---

### 5 · THEN COMES SAARTHI — 9s  *(warm bloom — lift your energy here)*
> "Until now. **Saarthi** is one AI companion that **finds** Ramesh, **onboards** him in one chat, **funds** his shop, grows his wealth, and **protects** him for life — all on **WhatsApp, in Hindi**. Watch."

*Captions:* Then comes Saarthi → Finds · Onboards · Funds · Protects → On WhatsApp. In Hindi. → Watch.

---

### 6 · LIVE PROTOTYPE — 48s  *(the live demo plays; narrate over it, matching the on-screen agent captions)*
> "**Pehchaan** finds him from his own UPI and GST data. **Ek Baat-cheet** opens his account in a single Hindi chat — no forms, no branch. **Punji** sanctions three lakh rupees in ten seconds. Then **Aadat** spots his idle cash and sends him to **Sikho** — where he practises investing with **play money**, risk-free — before **Nivesh** starts a real SIP. **Zindagi Radar** sees Diwali coming and offers a goal fund for his daughter. And when a scam arrives, **Kavach** blocks it — and explains why, **in Hindi**."

*On-screen agent captions auto-rotate:* Pehchaan → Ek Baat-cheet → Punji → Aadat/Sikho → Nivesh → Zindagi Radar → Kavach
*Tip:* pace your narration so each agent's line lands as its caption appears (~one every 6–7s).

---

### 7 · ARCHITECTURE — 11s  *(warm, confident)*
> "Behind the conversation: **one avatar, six agents, one shared memory** — sitting on **Account Aggregator**, **Bhashini**, and **SBI's own loan engine**. Three layers under every agent: **speak, learn, protect.**"

*Captions:* One avatar · six agents → One shared memory → On AA · Bhashini · SBI's engine → Speak · Learn · Protect

---

### 8 · WHATSAPP + YONO — 9s
> "WhatsApp is the **front door** — it reaches people who don't have the app yet. And the same brain **plugs into YONO 2.0**. One agent, two doorways — **not competing, completing.**"

*Captions:* WhatsApp — the front door → Plugs into YONO 2.0 → One brain, two doorways

---

### 9 · TRUST — 9s  *(steady, reassuring — this is the jury-proofing beat)*
> "For a public bank, **trust is the product**. **Consented** Account Aggregator data. A **human in the loop** on every agent. And **anti-deepfake voice** on every transaction."

*Captions:* Consented AA data → Human-in-the-loop → Anti-deepfake voice

---

### 10 · WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT — 11s  *(build energy toward the close)*
> "What sets us apart? A **flight simulator** that kills the fear of losing money. A **fraud shield** that speaks plain Hindi. **Festival and life-event awareness** no global bank has. And **real products** — a loan, an SIP — completed live, not just talked about."

*Captions:* Sikho — practise, then real → Kavach — fraud you can hear → Festival-aware · India-native → Real products, completed live

---

### 11 · CLOSE — 9s  *(warm, slow, land it)*
> "From **invisible**, to **funded**, to **protected** — for life. **Saarthi.** Banking that **speaks your language.**"

*Captions:* Invisible → Funded → Protected → SBI Saarthi → Banking that speaks your language

---

## Recording checklist
1. Open `SBI_Saarthi_Pitch.html` in Chrome → press **`P`** (hide UI) → **`F`** (fullscreen).
2. Press **`Space`** to play; screen-record the whole run (OBS / `Win+G`).
3. In Canva/Filmora: drop the recording on the timeline, record or paste VO per scene above, add soft background music (~15% volume), and you're done.
4. If a scene's VO runs long, open the HTML and bump that `<section>`'s `data-dur` value (in seconds).
